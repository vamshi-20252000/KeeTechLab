TERM'S & CONDITIONS

1. Scope of Services

KeetechLabs provides IT development and support services, including but not limited to website development, mobile application development, hosting, and ongoing maintenance.

  • Basic Plan – ₹1,999/month: Portfolio website and app.
  • Medium Plan – ₹3,999/month: Small-scale e-commerce or office website and app.
  • Large Plan – ₹7,999/month: Larger-scale website and app.
  • Custom Plan – Priced based on specific requirements outside the above categories.

The services rendered under this Agreement include IT support, maintenance, and bug fixes but do not cover third-party costs (e.g., domain, hosting, SMS gateways, Firebase storage).

2. Payment Terms & Initial Deposit

  • The Client is required to pay a six-month subscription fee upfront as a security deposit before the commencement of services.
  • Monthly subscription payments commence from the first month, in addition to the security deposit.
  • Payment Due Date: The monthly subscription fee must be paid by the 5th of each month.
  • Grace Period: A 10-business-day grace period is provided, making the final deadline the 15th of each month.

3. Consequences of Non-Payment

  • Service Suspension: If payment is not received by the 15th of the month, KeetechLabs reserves the right to immediately suspend all services.
  • Termination: If non-payment persists for 30 days, KeetechLabs will terminate all services, including domain hosting, web/app access, and IT infrastructure.
  • Data Retention & Storage Fees: Client data will be stored for 60 days at no charge; afterward, a ₹590/month storage fee applies.
  • Legal Recourse: KeetechLabs reserves the right to initiate legal action for outstanding dues. The Client is liable for legal costs.

4. Domain, Hosting & Intellectual Property Rights

  • All domains, hosting, and app store listings (Google Play/App Store) shall be registered under KeetechLabs’ name for subscription clients.
  • KeetechLabs retains full control over all domain, hosting, and app deployment rights.
  • Upon cancellation, the Client must purchase domain and hosting rights separately after clearing outstanding dues.

5. Third-Party Charges & Billing

The subscription fee covers IT development and support but excludes third-party costs such as:

  • Domain registration and renewal fees
  • Office email services
  • SMS gateway and Firebase storage fees
  • Third-party APIs, cloud hosting, or external integrations

Invoices for these charges will be provided by the 10th of each month and must be settled by the 15th.

6. IT Support, Maintenance & Additional Modifications

  • KeetechLabs provides continuous IT support, maintenance, and bug fixes as part of the subscription.
  • Feature updates will be provided only if necessary for efficiency or security enhancements.
  • Custom modifications or new feature additions require separate one-time payments.

7. Subscription Cancellation & Refund Policy

  • Clients must provide a 30-day written notice for cancellation.
  • The security deposit will be refunded within 45 days, subject to the clearance of all outstanding dues.
  • Refunds shall be issued via NEFT to the Client’s registered bank account.

8. Data Ownership, Handover & Liability

  • Client data remains under KeetechLabs' control during the subscription period.
  • Data will only be released upon full payment of outstanding dues.
  • After 60 days post-cancellation, a ₹590/month storage fee applies.
  • Clients must sign an agreement before data is handed over.

9. Legal Compliance & Dispute Resolution

  • This Agreement is legally binding under Indian Contract Law.
  • Any dispute shall be resolved under the exclusive jurisdiction of Indian courts.
  • The Client indemnifies KeetechLabs against any claims, liabilities, or disputes.

10. Miscellaneous

  • KeetechLabs reserves the right to amend this Agreement with prior notice.
  • No refunds or service continuations shall be granted if the Client violates the Agreement.
  • Chargebacks, fraudulent claims, or legal violations will be treated as contractual fraud.

By subscribing to KeetechLabs' services, the Client agrees to all terms and conditions outlined above.
